Welcome to Vizag Startups

Captain your very own startup! Give wind to your dreams! Steer the startup waters with Vizag Startups by your side!

We are here to help!

We build a vibrant, robust ecosystem in this thriving academic and industrial port city so that you can build on your idea and startup. Let's get going!

1. Connect

Connect with like minded people! Our curated ecosystem includes dreamers, startups, co-working spaces, mentors, investors, students, accelerators, incubators, and government officials.

2. Startup!

You have dreams and ambitions! You have an idea that will benefit people! Get off the bench, put on your gear, and get to bat now! We are experienced and enthusiastic fellow dreamers eager to help aspiring startup ninjas to brainstorm, validate, and launch ideas.

3. Grow

Sartups come with a googol number of challenges! You know it, we know it. Our ecosystem is here to help you scale and pivot your way to success!

Community Learning

Knowledge evolves quickly. Stay abreast. Let's learn together! We meet online and offline to do courses, read books and much more. So take that book you always wanted to finish, read it, and discuss with peers.

Discussion Forums

We have thought provoking threads running for various domains and topics. From markets to payrolls to local regulations, everything related to startups is here.

Mentors

Need another perspective? An expert opinion? From early stage startups to those who are busy scaling, we have an expanding network of mentors willing to help you get to the next level.

Brainstorm & Ideate

Lot's of startups are born out of casual discussions and brainstorming sessions. Join our hyperactive Whatsapp groups suited to your interests and start brainstorming.

Prototype

Done with ideation? Get out there and build a prototype. Use our diverse and extensive resources to get your MVP out asap!

Launch

You won't know if your ship will sink or float until you launch. Start with a simple boat. Launch and launch fast! Launch at our meetups, get your early adopters, and valuable feedback.

Incubators

The starting stage is the most crucial for every startup. Collaborate with incubators who understand local entrepreneurs.

Accelerators

Done with idea validation? Scale and scale quick! Collaborate with local accelerators who know your pain points and can fuel your growth.

Investor Networks

Ready to sail but lack the cash? Pitch now! We proudly present a network of angel investors and VCs who are ready to fund viable startups.
